In September 2020, I entered the School of Social and Behavioral Sciences at the University of Amsterdam to study communication and will graduate as an honours student. My undergraduate thesis, supervised by Dr. Caroline Ishen, focused on virtual influencers on social media.

Since 2020, I have been running my own Redbook social media account as an influencer and have self-participated in several marketing campaigns. I also work part-time as a freelance model to fund my studies and conduct field-based social research as an Asian model working in the European fashion industry.

I am currently pursuing a minor in data science and artificial intelligence, drilling Python algorithms, database MySQL, R and machine learning. As such, my research interests lie at the intersection of human-computer interaction, social media advertising, and sociology.
